ACCOMMODATION

The 27th Edition of IEEE ISORC conference will be held in Mövenpick Hotel Gammarth Tunis, Carthage, Tunisia

Route from airport to hotel

  • The destination Airport is Tunis Carthage: Tunis Carthage Airport
  • Distance from the airport like 20 minutes. Route from airport to hotel

Travel & Taxi details:

    At your arrival to Tunis-Carthage Airport, if you need a taxi, you use the follwoing options:
  • On the first floor of the airport, you can take a yellow taxi to drop you off at your hotel.
  • You can also use the Bolt app to book a taxi to drop you off at your hotel. Bolt at Carthage Airport
  • If you need to rent a car, AVIS is a good option we recommend for you:

ACCOMMODATION

Participants who wish to reserve a room at the Movenpick Hotel Gammarth will benefit from the promotional rate granted for the conference. Please download the ISORC conférence 2024 Booking Form, fill it and sent it to the email addresses as described in the form. The hotel will send you a secure payment link to settle your booking fees.
