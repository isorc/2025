REGISTRATION INFORMATION

ISORC 2024 will be held in Mövenpick Hotel Gammarth Tunis, Carthage, Tunisia

See Travel details to have a discount when

Registration Rules:

  • An accepted paper will be published in the proceedings only if the final camera-ready version is accompanied by the payment of at least one AUTHOR Regular REGISTRATION type (either IEEE Member or Non - IEEE Member) for one of the authors (even if all authors of the paper are students, i.e., a student registration does not cover any paper).
  • Each regular registration covers a maximum of one paper.
  • IEEE reserves the right to exclude a paper from distribution after the conference, including IEEE Xplore Digital Library, if the paper is not presented (onsite or online) by the author at the conference.

  • One Extra Page (Max 2 pages for each paper): $100.

  • One Extra Gala Dinner Voucher $100, will be managed onsite
